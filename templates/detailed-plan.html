ACT I (Chapters 8)

The standard Lorem

Third person limited, focused on Sam Chevalier.

Sam discovers Juliette's brutally murdered body in Le Spectre Bleu after hours, shattering his carefully constructed world. Through grief-stricken flashbacks, we witness their passionate secret relationship and the club's atmosphere before this devastating discovery transforms Sam from neutral businessman to hunted man, as his violent nature surfaces in his grief.

Objective

Hook readers with immediate visceral tension through murder discovery while establishing Sam's character, his passionate relationship with Juliette, and the noir atmosphere of 1950s Montreal.

Character Development

Sam's controlled facade crumbles completely, revealing not only his capacity for love and loss, but also the violent potential he's inherited from his criminal family, setting up his transformation arc.

Pacing

Atmospheric opening building to shocking discovery, then explosive emotional breakdown before slowing for strategic flashback revelation, ending with mounting dread.
