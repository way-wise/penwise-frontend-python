Genre Analysis Framework

Fiction Genres

Romance: Largest category, highly engaged readers, 50k-90k words

Mystery/Thriller: Series-driven, plot-focused, 70k-90k words

Sci-Fi/Fantasy: World-building intensive, series expected, 80k-150k words

Nonfiction Categories

Business/Self-Help: Platform crucial, 40k-70k words

Health/Fitness: Credentials important, visual elements, 50k-80k words

Memoir: Unique story essential, 70k-100k words

Query Letter Structure

Opening Hook (1-2 sentences): Most compelling aspect of your book
Story Synopsis (150-250 words): Main character, goal, obstacles, stakes
Book Details: Title, genre, word count, series info
Comparable Titles: Two recent books with similar audience
Author Bio: Relevant experience, platform, credentials
